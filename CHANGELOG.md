### Breaking Changes

* APIs don't accept callbacks anymore, instead, they return a Promise now
  * `viewer#importXML`
  * `viewer#importDefinitions`
  * `viewer#open`
  * `viewer#saveXML`
  * `viewer#saveSVG`
  * `modeler#createDiagram`
  * `importer#importlandscapeDiagram`
